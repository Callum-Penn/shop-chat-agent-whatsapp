# Feature Summary - Shopify AI Chat Agent

This document outlines the key features and improvements added to the AI chat experience from a customer's perspective.

---

## Core Chat Experience

### Chat Reset Button

• Reset button in the chat input area  
• Starts a new conversation with one click  
• Clears history without losing your session  
• Useful for switching between shopping queries

### Notification Sound

• Plays when the bot sends a new message  
• Sound plays whether the chat window is open or closed  
• Simple, non-intrusive  
• No additional setup required

### Unread Message Counter

• Red badge on the chat bubble when closed  
• Shows the number of unread messages  
• Clears when the chat is opened  
• Updates in real time

### Cookie Persistence

• Chat history persists across sessions  
• Return to your conversation after closing the browser  
• Works across devices when logged in  
• 90-day retention

---

## Smart Features

### Quantity Increment Validation

• Bot checks product minimums before adding to cart  
• Explains quantity rules (e.g., must order in 5s)  
• Automatically rounds up (7 → 10)  
• Avoids checkout errors by enforcing rules upfront  
• Upsells when an increment provides better value

### Automatic Follow-Up Messages

• First follow-up after 3 minutes: "It looks like you may have stepped away..."  
• Second follow-up after 5 minutes: WhatsApp community invite  
• Triggered when idle  
• Resets if you reply

---

## Customer Service Integration

### Customer Service Handoff via Email

• Request to speak with a person  
• Bot collects name, email, and phone  
• Sends your conversation summary to the team  
• Team email includes key details  
• Ends the chat after handoff to avoid duplicates

---

## WhatsApp Integration

### WhatsApp Chat Banner

• Banner appears at the top of the chat window  
• "Chat on WhatsApp instead?"  
• One tap to switch  
• Optional; you can continue on web

### Spreadsheet Order System

• Request a bulk-order spreadsheet template  
• Pre-filled (best sellers) or blank form  
• Fill in products and quantities  
• Send back via WhatsApp  
• Supports large orders

---

## Broadcast Communications

### Broadcast Center

• Send to all users via WhatsApp, web chat, or both  
• Rich formatting: heading, image, and message  
• Images stored securely and display inline  
• Results and delivery tracking  
• Suitable for announcements, promotions, and updates

---

## Message Formatting

### Image Rendering

• Inline images in messages and broadcasts  
• Rounded corners  
• Responsive sizing  
• Supports JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP  
• Displays immediately

### Markdown Support

• Bold text for emphasis  
• Clickable links  
• Numbered and bullet lists  
• Clean, readable formatting  
• Links open in new tabs

---

## Cross-Device Experience

### Customer Cross-Device Linking

• One account across devices  
• Conversation history syncs when logged in  
• Continue on mobile or desktop  
• Consistent experience

---

## Summary

The chat now includes notifications, handoffs, visual messages, smarter quantity handling, WhatsApp support, and persistent history across devices.
